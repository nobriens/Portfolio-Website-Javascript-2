Peer2Pal

An app that allows EV owners to rent out their chargers to other EV users. Connecting people looking for a charge with nearby,
available stations, finding an EV charger is more convenient and accessible.

The Challenge

Peer 2 Pal was developed in my 3rd year at university for ESB, Irelands leading electrical provider. The app was intended to promote a breakthrough in communities by encouraging people to connect and see the benefits of being in an independent peer to peer community. The app also helps to build up a better network amongst peers and pals which will take the strain off of the main EV charging infrastructure while also giving EV users more freedom with the ability to charge almost anywhere and offer the usage of their personal charging stations at their own desired rate.
